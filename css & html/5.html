4 Diseases that Often Attack on a Mountain

  • Return Menu

    • 1. Hypothermia, Simply put, hypothermia is an event where body temperature rises continuously unnoticed. This disease can attack when wet conditions in cold and windy areas. First aid is changing wet clothes with dry ones, getting into a sleeping bag, and filling with warm food and drinks.

    2. AMS Acute Mountain Sickness (AMS) is an altitude disease that often occurs at an altitude of 3,000 - 4,000 masl. Symptoms include headache, nausea, loss of appetite, shortness of breath, to sleep disorders. To overcome this, climbers must remain aware and do light activities. Don't try to climb any higher if this symptom has arisen.

    3.Frostbite, Frostbite atau radang dingin adalah kondisi di mana jaringan tubuh membeku dan rusak oleh paparan suhu rendah. Peradangan ini biasa terjadi pada tangan, kaki, hidung, dan telinga. Penyakit ini dapat memakan waktu beberapa minggu untuk pulih. Bahkan penderita dapat kehilangan kulit, jari, dan kaki serta cacat jika tak segera ditangani.

    4.Heat Stroke, Heat stroke is the opposite of hypthermia. The condition of the body experiences a dramatic increase in body temperature in a fast time. Heat strokes usually occur when a person feels extreme heat due to exposure to heat from sunburn outside the body's tolerance limits. The most easily seen symptom is high fever reaching 40 degrees. Facing nature cannot be done haphazardly. Although climbing mountains seems easy, it takes a lot of knowledge to be able to survive at altitude.
